About Nexus

CEC Nexus is a vibrant community fostering strong alumni relations, connecting graduates across generations with current students and the institution. Through mentorship, networking, and shared opportunities, Nexus bridges the gap between experience and aspiration. Together, we grow, collaborate, and shape the future of the CEC family.
